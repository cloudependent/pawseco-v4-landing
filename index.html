Security & Compliance — baked in

PawsEco is engineered for multi‑tenant safety from the database up. We align our controls to recognised frameworks and keep responsibilities crystal‑clear for merchants and partners.

Tenant isolation

  • • PostgreSQL Row‑Level Security (RLS)
  • • RBAC roles & fine‑grained permissions
  • • Per‑tenant IDs & scoped APIs

App hardening

  • • JWT auth (short‑lived access + refresh)
  • • TLS everywhere; strict CORS
  • • Least‑privilege service configs

Audit & continuity

  • • Structured audit logs for key actions
  • • Backups & tested restore procedures
  • • Error tracking & rate‑limit guards

Standards alignment (what we design for)

ISO 27001
Access control, operations security, supplier management mapping.
SOC 2
Security, availability, processing integrity practices.
GDPR/CCPA
Data rights, retention, deletion requests support.
PCI‑DSS aware
Card data handled by certified processors; no raw PAN storage in PawsEco.

Note: “Aligned” indicates our controls are designed to support these frameworks. Formal certification/compliance may vary by deployment and merchant obligations.

  • • Dependency updates and vulnerability scanning
